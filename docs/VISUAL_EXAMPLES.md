## Real-World Example

### Complete Kitchen BEO Flow

1. **Event Planner** creates event in system
2. **Executive Chef** fills in menu details
3. **Sous Chef** adds prep schedule
4. **System** validates data with Zod schemas
5. **PDF Generator** creates Kitchen BEO PDF
6. **System** emails PDF to kitchen staff
7. **Kitchen Team** prints and uses during event
8. **Staff** checks off tasks as completed
9. **Post-Event** PDF archived with completion notes

### Complete Service BEO Flow

1. **Event Manager** creates service plan
2. **Event Captain** fills in timeline and staff
3. **System** validates and generates Service BEO PDF
4. **System** emails to FOH team and vendors
5. **Staff** reviews before event
6. **During Event** staff follows timeline
7. **Post-Event** actual times noted
8. **System** archives for future reference
